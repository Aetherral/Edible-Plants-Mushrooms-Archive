White Clover

Scientific name: Trifolium repens

Information

ever since I started recognizing the White clover for what it is, so many possibilities have opened up to me. These things are everywhere, my lawn, everyone else's lawn, every major field I have seen.
many think these to be a weed, but it is clear to me that that cannot be further from the truth.

The White Clover is found in literally every US state, all but one province of Canada, and Greenland, It is native to europe and is the result of a crossbreed between two earlier clover species.

in addition to being edible, it also can be used to make a healing ointment, being able to help heal small cuts, wounds, sores, and burns

Creeping Wood Sorrel

Scientific name: Oxalis corniculata

Information

The creeping wood sorrel is a variant of the Wood sorrel, the difference? location. It is found in more shady places, specifically along the bottom of walls.
here is a photo that I took:
this one is not fully grown by the way, so when fully grown it would likely have larger leaves

The Wood Sorrel is actually native to all but 6 states in the united states, these being: Nevada, california, Idaho, Hawaii, and Alaska

This one can also be eaten raw, and has a somewhat lemony taste.from what I can tell, it is basically the same in structure as the regular wood sorrel so all of the same recipes work
